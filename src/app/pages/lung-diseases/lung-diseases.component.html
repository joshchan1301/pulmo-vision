Normal Lung ▼

Healthy lungs with no visible abnormalities. X-ray appears clear without unusual spots or opacities.

Bacterial Pneumonia ▼
  • Causes: Most commonly by Streptococcus pneumoniae and other bacteria.
  • Symptoms: Cough, fever, chest pain, shortness of breath.
  • Complications: Pleural effusion, sepsis, respiratory failure.
  • Prevention: Vaccination, good hygiene, avoiding smoking.
  • Treatment: Usually antibiotics, supportive care (oxygen, fluids).
Viral Pneumonia ▼
  • Causes: Influenza, RSV, SARS-CoV-2 (COVID-19), and other viruses.
  • Symptoms: Cough, fever, fatigue, shortness of breath.
  • Complications: May lead to secondary bacterial infection, ARDS.
  • Prevention: Flu vaccination, masks, avoid contact with infected people.
  • Treatment: Antiviral meds (sometimes), mainly supportive care.
