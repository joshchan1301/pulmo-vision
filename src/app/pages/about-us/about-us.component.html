Pulmo
Vision

Pulmo Vision is an AI-powered web application designed to assist in the early detection of lung diseases through chest X-ray analysis. This project was developed as part of a graduation thesis, with the goal of applying state-of-the-art computer vision technologies to real-world medical challenges.

The system leverages advanced deep learning models such as Vision Transformer (ViT) and Swin Transformer, combined with Grad-CAM visualization techniques to highlight suspicious regions in the lungs. Users can simply upload a chest X-ray image, and within seconds, the AI analyzes it and provides a diagnosis‚Äîclassifying it as Normal, Bacterial Pneumonia, or Viral Pneumonia‚Äîalong with relevant suggestions.

Pulmo Vision is intended to serve as a supportive tool for students, researchers, and medical professionals, especially in low-resource settings where access to advanced diagnostic tools like CT or MRI is limited. While not a replacement for professional diagnosis, this tool demonstrates the potential of AI in augmenting medical imaging workflows.

WHY PULMO VISION MATTERS

OBJECTIVE

  • Enhance the accuracy and speed of preliminary lung disease screening.
  • Provide a user-friendly web-based interface for real-time analysis.
  • Reduce dependency on high-cost imaging modalities like CT or MRI in low-resource settings.

SIGNIFICANCE

  • Lung diseases remain one of the leading causes of illness and death worldwide, especially in areas with limited medical resources.
  • This project demonstrates how artificial intelligence‚Äîspecifically computer vision models‚Äîcan be integrated into accessible web platforms to assist in medical image interpretation.
  • A tool for students to explore medical-AI integration.
  • A demonstration of explainable AI using Grad-CAM to highlight suspicious lung regions.
  • A proof-of-concept showing that modern AI can support, not replace, medical professionals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pulmo Vision?
Pulmo Vision is a web application that uses artificial intelligence (AI) to analyze chest X-ray images and assist in the early detection of lung diseases such as bacterial and viral pneumonia.
How accurate is the model?
The current model achieves a test accuracy of approximately 90‚Äì95% on the validation dataset. However, it is not intended to replace a professional medical diagnosis.
Does the website store my X-ray images?
No, all X-ray images are processed in real-time and are not stored after delivering the results.
Can Pulmo Vision replace traditional diagnostic methods?
No, Pulmo Vision is intended for educational and research purposes only, and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice or clinical diagnosis.
